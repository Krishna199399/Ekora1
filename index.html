Company Overview

EGC was founded to revolutionize cosmetic product launch lifecycles. We combine top-tier pharmaceutical-grade chemical research with automated robotics and industrial cleanroom layout design.

Today, EGC serves as the key technical partner for major skincare conglomerates, emerging D2C brands, and dermatological clinical networks across five continents.

COMMON QUERIES

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for custom cosmetic formula development? +

Typically, laboratory bench prototype development takes 4 to 6 weeks. Following client prototype validation, we initiate standard 3-month accelerated stability testing. In total, complete formulation development takes around 4 months.

Do you assist in packaging sourcing and supplier identification? +

Yes. We have a robust global supplier network for custom luxury glass containers, sustainable wood-derived closures, airless pumps, and cardboard box printers. We manage full packaging coordination, compatibility trials, and leak assessments.

What are the core requirements to set up a licensed cosmetic factory in India? +

To acquire a CDSCO cosmetic manufacturing license, you need a GMP-compliant factory layout, a dedicated raw material weighing room, compounding vessel zone, clean filling and packing room, and a QA laboratory. HVAC air circulation must meet BIS guidelines. EGC handles the entire engineering layout planning and licensing dossiers.

Does EGC assist with third-party contract manufacturing coordination? +

Yes. If you prefer not to build a factory, we can identify and audit third-party contract manufacturers (OEM/ODM/Private Label) that match your batch size, quality requirements, and target cost-per-unit milestones.
